Who We Are
About VMC

The Victorian Milers Club is Victoria's premier middle-distance racing series, dedicated to the 800m, 1500m, and Mile. We provide a competitive, graded racing environment for athletes of all abilities — from first-time racers to Olympians.

VMC meets are held across Melbourne throughout the season at Lakeside Stadium, Box Hill, Aberfeldie, and Doncaster. Each meet features multiple grades so every athlete competes at their level.

Purpose
Mission Statement

"To provide the highest quality middle-distance racing in Victoria — accessible to all abilities, honouring the tradition of the mile, and fostering a community built on authentic competition."

Competition
Rules & Regulations
01
All athletes must be registered with Athletics Australia or an affiliated international body. Entry closes the Monday prior to each meet at 12pm.
02
Graded heats are assigned based on submitted estimated times. Athletes are responsible for providing accurate estimates.
03
Pacemakers must apply separately and are subject to committee approval. Pacers must withdraw before the bell lap if still leading.
04
Season points are awarded to the top 8 finishers in each heat. Best 4 results from 6 meets count toward final standings.
05
Club championship points are aggregated from all members' individual points.
06
All decisions by the VMC committee regarding results and points are final.
Race Support
Pacemaking

Pacemaking is a specialist role at VMC meets. Experienced runners are selected to set target paces for the top-grade races and must be able to deliver consistent splits while helping the field run fast and even.

Pacemakers are not entered as regular competitors. They must apply separately through the VMC pacemaker application process and receive committee approval before a meet.

Once assigned, pacemakers are required to follow the designated pace plan and to step off the track before the final bell lap if still leading the race. This ensures a fair and competitive finish for all athletes.

For more information or to express interest in pacemaking, please contact our events team at events@vicmilers.com or visit the entry page for pacemaker applications.
